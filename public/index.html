INFO

I’m a Software Engineer living in New Delhi, India, originally from Uttarakhand, India.

Since childhood, I have had a keen passion for computers. I was always fascinated by how computers worked and how data flowed from one place to another. It felt like magic to me! Whenever I had free time outside of my schoolwork, I delved into studying how networking functions in computers. However, I faced a challenge as there was no computer facility available to me at that time for practical experience.

Despite this, my curiosity and dedication to learning about computer networking never waned. After gaining decent knowledge about networking and the web, I built my first website on Wix. Following that, I created some websites using HTML and CSS, which boosted my confidence. Since then, I have never looked back and continuously learn new technologies and programming languages to stay up-to-date with current market needs. This passion ultimately led me to pursue a career in software engineering, where I continue to explore and grow my knowledge every day.
